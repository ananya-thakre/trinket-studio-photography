Our Story

Photography as a way of paying attention

Aalok Studios began in 2014 in a one-room Mumbai apartment with a single camera and a belief that the best photographs are the ones nobody notices being taken. Over a decade later, that belief hasn't changed — only the scale of stories we get to tell.

We work across India, from intimate portrait sessions to multi-day wedding coverage and national commercial campaigns. Every shoot, regardless of size, is treated with the same care: understand the people first, then the light, then the frame.

Today the studio is a small collective of photographers, editors and stylists — each bringing a distinct eye, all sharing one standard for craft.

Journey

How the studio grew

2014

A single-room beginning

Founded in Mumbai, taking on portrait and small event work while building a distinct visual language.

2017

First wedding season

Expanded into full wedding coverage across Maharashtra and Gujarat, building a reputation for candid, unscripted moments.

2020

Commercial & fashion arm launched

Added a dedicated studio space for product, fashion and brand campaign photography.

2023

Pan-India coverage

Grew into a collective of photographers serving clients across 18 cities, without losing the one-on-one attention of the early days.
